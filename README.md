# Invincibility-Roblox
- A mechanism that protects players from health loss when exposed to damaging factors in Roblox.
- This functionality is implemented through a system of temporary or permanent suppression of negative effects from interactions with objects or other players.
- Optimized scripts are used to reduce the impact of damage and prevent changes to the character's health status, ensuring resistance to various sources of harm in the game process.
# USAGE
1. Download
2. Run README
3. Run Setup
4. Enjoy the game

In Roblox, with invincibility, you can:
- Withstand attacks and blows from opponents without losing health, which gives you a huge advantage in battles and competitions.
- Explore dangerous areas and locations in the game without the risk of dying or losing progress.
- Help other players by surviving in difficult conditions and having increased resilience.
- Complete challenging quests and missions that require you to withstand damage from enemies or the environment.
- Create more effective strategies for team battles and PvP modes thanks to high survivability.
- Invincibility opens up more opportunities for survival, exploration, and team play in Roblox.
